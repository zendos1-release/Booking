BookPal

An app for people who like books.

BookPal is a reading companion running on Google Books API. You can use it to search for a book and add it to your reading list. While you read you can track your bookmarks or add some notes.

Over time BookPal will help you keep track of what you've read, how it made you feel and whether you liked it or not.

Use the app whatever way works for you. And reach out if you have any thoughts or feedback. Enjoy.
