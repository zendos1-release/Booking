BookPal

An app for people who like books.

BookPal is a reading companion running on Google Books API. You can use it to search for a book and add it to your reading list. While you read you can track your bookmarks or add some notes to keep track of your thoughts. Or you could add a review or a rating for a book you've just finished.

Over time BookPal will help you to keep track of what you've read, how it made you feel and whether you liked it or not. The app has various features for sorting and filtering your reading list, for adding books you'd like to read or just searching for books, to see what you want to read.

Use the app whatever way works for you. And reach out to us if you have any thoughts or feedback. Enjoy.
